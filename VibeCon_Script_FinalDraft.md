## 5 MOMENTS THAT WIN OR LOSE THIS

**1. "It's live." (0:22)**
Two-second pause after. Complete silence. Do not fill it.
That silence is confidence. Filling it is nerves.

**2. "...before I was even born." (1:27)**
Say "Because my parents had prayed for it there..." — then stop — full beat — "...before I was even born."
This is your only purely human moment in the video. Do not rush through it.

**3. Results page — 94% match (1:50)**
Cursor stops. Two full seconds. No words.
Let them read "Kurichi Mariyamman Temple. Nagapattinam."
That silence is the demo's strongest line.

**4. "800 million people. Nobody has built it." (2:18)**
Say them as two separate sentences with a full stop between.
Do not run them together. The pause after "800 million people" is what makes the next sentence land.

**5. Hard cut at the end (3:00)**
Stop the recording the instant "room" leaves your mouth.
No smile. No exhale. No thank you.
The hard stop is the final signal that you mean everything you just said.
